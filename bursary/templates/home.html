About CDF Bursaries

Welcome to the Constituency Development Fund (CDF) Bursaries Program — a dedicated initiative committed to enhancing education accessibility and empowering students across Kenyan schools. Our mission is to foster academic excellence, reduce dropout rates, and create a brighter future for the youth.

Key Objectives:

  • Promoting Education Access
  • Reducing Dropout Rates
  • Supporting Academic Excellence

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the CDF Bursaries, students must meet certain criteria, including:

  • Demonstrated financial need.
  • Satisfactory academic performance.
  • Commitment to their studies and community.
